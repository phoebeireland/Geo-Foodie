Welcome to Geo Foodie!

Have you ever found yourself looking for that perfect international dish to make for dinner to impress your family or do you just want to spice things up and make your average Thursday a bit more interesting? Have you found that simply looking recipes up in Google is far too daunting- there are so many countries to choose from and then so many delicious looking local recipes for each country. Perhaps you don't even know what you're hungry for. Well, look no further- Geo Foodie is here to help! Simply press the button, and a random recipe from a random country will pop up on your screen. No more hours of searching the intenet looking for something that maybe looks good. Geo Foodie takes out all of the deliberation by presenting you with a direct link to a delicious and authentic recipe.
